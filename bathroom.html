Bathroom Clean

What is included in Bathroom Cleaning?

Bathroom cleaning service includes
  • Cleaning the exterior of bathroom cabinets
  • Wiping the mirrors clean
  • Washing sinks,tiles,and faucets
  • Disinfecting the toilet, tub, shower and bathroom floor.
Regular follow-up cleanings are lighter and help you to avoid the mildew, mold, soap scum, and other buildup that can quickly occur in busy bathrooms.SBricks offers service providers having experience & expertise to provide the highest quality in bathroom cleaning and free the bathrooms from foul odors.
